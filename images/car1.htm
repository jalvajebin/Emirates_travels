---------------- About Our Company ----------------

Smart Way Limousine: Your Trusted Abu Dhabi Limousine Provider.

Smart Way Limousine is a distinguished passenger transportation company with a rich 16-year history in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) and a growing presence in Oman. Established in 2008, our headquarters is strategically located in Abu Dhabi. Over the years, we have built a stellar reputation as a reliable and successful entity, committed to delivering an exceptional and luxurious travel experience to our valued clients.

---------------- How It Works? ----------------

We Make All The Process Easy

STEP 01

Booking and Reservation

  • You contact us to book a ride through phone, email, or our website.
  • We gather details like when and where you need the ride and the type of car you want.
  • We create a reservation for your ride.

STEP 02

Vehicle Selection and Confirmation

  • We show you a range of cars from our fleet.
  • You pick the one that suits your needs, whether it's a sedan, van, or limousine.
  • We confirm your choice and provide all the details, including the driver's name.

STEP 03

Professional Chauffeur and Transportation

  • On the day, our professional driver arrives at your location early, typically 10 minutes before.
  • They are polite and well-dressed, with extensive training in safe driving and service.
  • They help with your things, open doors, and make sure your journey is comfortable.

STEP 04

Journey and Client Satisfaction

  • During your ride, enjoy luxury, comfort, and even some extras like climate control and entertainment.
  • Tell us how we did. We value your feedback to keep improving our service.
  • Pay as agreed, either right away, at the end, or with a prior arrangement.
